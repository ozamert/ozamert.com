Lisbon in March means mild weather and no crowds. Alfama for the azulejo facades and the smell of grilled sardines at noon. LX Factory on Sunday for the market.

## Getting around

Tram 28 is genuinely useful and not just a tourist trap — it connects Alfama to Chiado efficiently. The steep hills mean the trams are slow; allow extra time.

## What surprised me

The light. The Atlantic diffuses it differently than Mediterranean cities. Golden hour lasts noticeably longer and the tiles glow.

Clean chord changes are the wall every beginner hits. Here's the daily 20-min that broke through.

## Routine

- 5 min: finger stretches + chromatic warmup
- 10 min: 4-chord rotation (G · D · Em · C) — focus on transition, not speed
- 5 min: play one song you love badly

## What changed

Week 1: transitions take 2 seconds. Week 4: 0.5 seconds. The trick is slow + clean, not fast + sloppy.

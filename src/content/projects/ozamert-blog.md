Personal blog scaffolded with Astro + MDX, designed in open-design, content drafted by local LLMs (Qwen + DeepSeek via Ollama).

## Stack

- Astro + MDX content collections
- Inter font, single-family typography
- Local LLM prompt library in `scripts/prompts/`
- open-design MCP for OG images and social carousels
- Deploy: Vercel

First episode of what I'm calling Mert Talks Data — short, practical episodes about data engineering and ML in the real world.

This pilot covers how I built ozamert.com: the Astro + MDX stack, why I chose it over a Notion-hosted blog, and the AI agent workflow I set up to draft posts with local LLMs (Qwen2.5-Coder via Ollama).
